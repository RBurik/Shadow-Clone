Felipe Cisneros

Felipe Cisneros is a writer and director from Sacramento California. His love for film influenced him to take acting and improv in college with the hopes of becoming an actor.

But eventually he found his way to screenwriting and directing where he discovered his true calling. Through vast research and studying his idols, Quentin Tarantino and Robert Rodriguez, he taught himself the craft by getting on set and creating a series of short films.

In 2019, he made his feature directorial debut down in Baja, Mexico where he shot on a tight 16 day schedule and 30 plus crew to get the job done. His Feature film has since been picked up by Gravitas Ventures set for a March 2021 release.
